Nutritious Meals for Growing Children

The quality of the ingredients, preparation and taste of meals is so important your little wonder’s physical and mental well-being. We take great pride in our nutritionally-balanced menus to make sure all children have the opportunity to try new foods and experience a wide range of cultural meals that incorporate a wide range of vegetables and fruit with a focus on low added sugar and unsaturated fats.

Much like at home or in a cafe, our menus are rotated frequently to expand young palates and we cater to a variety of dietary and cultural requirements. We work in consultation with families who have individual allergy requirements and our premises and procedures comply with all food safety regulations.

Our menus are developed using the Australian Dietary Guidelines and the Australian Guide To Healthy Eating, and are consistent with the recommendations in the Australian Government Get Up & Grow: Healthy eating and physical activity for early childhood. We are food lovers and we only make food we would want to eat ourselves!

Our Nutrition Philosophy

Fresh & Local Ingredients

Sourced from local Ballarat farmers and suppliers, supporting our community and ensuring maximum freshness.

Seasonal Menus

Rotating seasonal menus that introduce children to variety, new flavors, and fresh produce year-round.

No Processed Foods

Everything made from scratch - no pre-packaged or processed foods, preservatives, or artificial additives.

Dietary Requirements

Accommodating allergies, intolerances, cultural preferences, and vegetarian/vegan diets with care and respect.

Educational Experience

Children participate in cooking activities, learning about nutrition, food origins, and healthy choices.

Family-Style Dining

Meals served family-style, encouraging social interaction, independence, and positive mealtime experiences.

Kitchen Features & Standards

5-Star Food Safety Rating

Certified with highest food safety standards, regular health inspections, and strict hygiene protocols.

Qualified Chef

Experienced chef with pediatric nutrition training and early childhood food safety certification.

Commercial Equipment

State-of-the-art kitchen with commercial-grade appliances, ensuring safe, efficient food preparation.

Allergy Management

Comprehensive allergy protocols, separate preparation areas, and clear labeling for all dietary needs.

Daily Fresh Preparation

All meals prepared fresh daily on-site - nothing frozen, reheated, or prepared in advance.

Temperature Monitoring

Digital temperature logging, proper food storage, and safe reheating procedures for optimal food safety.
