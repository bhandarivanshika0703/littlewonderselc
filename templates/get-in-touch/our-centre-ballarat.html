Find Us in Ballarat

Little Wonders Early Learning has a community-first approach to life in regional Victoria. Shane and Natalie create a homely atmosphere that ensures all the little wonders have a smooth transition from home into care, and supports the holistic well-being of all their children. We’re open from 6:30am till 6:30pm, Monday to Friday 52 weeks a year, closed on public holidays.
